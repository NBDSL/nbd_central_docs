Visualize thorugh vpn or ssh
==================================

To render&visualize GUIs of any software (maestro, vmd, pymol ...) you must follow the next steps:


1- Activate the vpn with your password

2- Connect to  https://172.16.1.3:28443/enginframe/vdi/ with your normal useraccount&password

3- On the left panel go to applications>Linux Desktop

4- Double click on the new linux desktop that will appear in the central panel

5- You will enter in the nbdcalc01 computer where you can visualize and calculate schordinger jobs
